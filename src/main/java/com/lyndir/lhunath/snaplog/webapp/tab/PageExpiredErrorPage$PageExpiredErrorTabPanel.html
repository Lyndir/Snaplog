You've gone idle!

You haven't been active on the site.
You've been logged out of your session and will have to restart.

What's going on?

When you don't use the site for several minutes we take the precaution of shutting down your session by automatically logging you out.
We do this because we can't be certain of whether the user that begins to use the site again after a period of inactivity is really you or somebody else making use of your open pages while you've gone away for a while.
